Steps to follow

Are you a student?

Step 1

Open the MyHostel app and continue as a student

Step 2

Search through the map and view the different hostel locations around Makerere Universiy

Step 3

Tap on your hostel of interest to view room specifications and prices

Step 4

Satisfied? Book a room and wait for a confirmation from the hostel custodian


Are you a custodian?

Step 1

Open the MyHostel app and login as a custodian.

Step 2

New to MyHostel? Register your hostel,and fill in the required detils

Step 3

Already on MyHostel? Simply login

Step 4

Set and modify room prices and availability
